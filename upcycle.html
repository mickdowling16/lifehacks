Jam Jar Terrarium

Submitted by Alex T

There are a whole host of crafty ideas out there to give glass jam jars a new lease of life, but have you considered bringing the outdoors in with your own terrarium? All you’ll need are pebbles for drainage (collecting and choosing these is great fun for little ones), a small amount of soil or compost and a small, hardy house plant – an air plant, succulent or cactus works well. Then get building!

Upcycle Old Mirrors To Be The Centre of The Room

Submitted by Sarah G

A mirror can be an excellent base for a variety of looks. If you have a number of left over compact mirrors from old makeup and jewllery boxes, pull them out of the compact and start collecting them. Once you have a small supply, they can be combined into unique wall art. Simply arrange them in a pattern and secure them to a base. Then, hang it on your wall to create a point of interest.
